# The Forexecutioner — Deploy in 10 Minutes

Five files. One upload. An icon on your home screen. Then you dial.

## Deploy to GitHub Pages

1. Go to github.com → New repository. Name it `forexecutioner`. Public. Create.
2. On the empty repo page, click "uploading an existing file". Drag in all 5 files from this folder:
   - index.html
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - icon-180.png
   - icon-512.png
3. Commit.
4. Repo → Settings → Pages → Source: "Deploy from a branch" → Branch: main, folder: / (root) → Save.
5. Wait ~1 minute. Your app is live at: `https://YOURUSERNAME.github.io/forexecutioner/`

## Install on iPhone

1. Open that URL in Safari on your phone.
2. Share button → Add to Home Screen → Add.
3. Open it from the icon. Full screen, works offline, feels native.

## Your data

- Everything lives on your phone, in the app's own storage. Nothing is sent anywhere.
- Home-screen apps on iOS keep their storage indefinitely. Still: **Ledger tab → Export everything** once a week. Thirty seconds. Your reps are the asset.
- New phone someday: export on the old one, import on the new one.

## The daily loop

Dawn (5 min) → Hunt (live tallies while dialing) → Card after real conversations (90 sec) → Dusk (5 min, close the day, bank the XP).

Low day? There's a low-day close button. A closed bad day beats an unclosed good one. The streak measures showing up to the mirror, not performance.

## The deal

Fewer than 3 cards filed in the first 5 days means the mirror is being avoided,
and that conversation matters more than this app.
